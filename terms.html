What Pigge is

Pigge is a tool that helps groups split restaurant bills. You or someone else scans a receipt, everyone claims what they ordered, and Pigge calculates each person's share including tax and tip.

Pigge does not process payments. When you tap "Pay via Venmo" (or any other payment method) in Pigge, we deep-link you to that third-party app. We never hold your money, never move money between accounts, and are not a money transmitter. Any money you send flows directly between you and the recipient through their payment app.
